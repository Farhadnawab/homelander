The Homelander Story.

When the Einhorn's made an Aliyah from America some time ago, it was the most incredible moment of their life until reality set in. The house was bare & the kids were hungry. There were no appliances and they were missing basics like a mop, broom or shower curtain. Overwhelmed and exhausted, Moshe vowed to help other olim have a smoother journey.

Enter Homelander. What started as a passion project helping and advising soon-to-be Israelis turned into a full-scale service benefiting many. From when a family first starts thinking about making Aliyah, to the moment they land and beyond, Homelander is there every step of the way, ensuring a smooth, pleasant transition for all.
